About the Event

The School Festival is a celebration of culture, talent, and community. Join us to experience a day full of performances, exhibitions, and fun activities!

Schedule

Location: School Auditorium and Grounds

  • 10:00 AM - Opening Ceremony
  • 11:00 AM - Art and Science Exhibitions
  • 1:00 PM - Cultural Performances
  • 3:00 PM - Sports Competitions
  • 5:00 PM - Closing Ceremony

Audience Info

The festival is open to students, families, and the local community. Free entry for all attendees. Make sure to bring your enthusiasm and energy!

Volunteer or Donate

Support our event by volunteering or making a donation. Volunteers will assist with event coordination, while donations will help us create an unforgettable experience.
